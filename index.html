Bounce Master

Welcome to the Bounce Master! Your objective is to keep the ball bouncing within the game area by controlling the paddle. Each successful bounce increases your score. Let's see how long you can keep the ball in play!

How to Play:

  • Move your mouse left and right to control the paddle.
  • The ball will bounce off the walls and the paddle.
  • Each time the ball hits the paddle, your score increases.
  • The game ends if the ball falls below the paddle.

Game Features:

  • Simple yet addictive gameplay.
  • Aesthetic gradient background for a visually pleasing experience.
  • Responsive paddle control using mouse movements.
  • Keep track of your score and challenge yourself to achieve a high score!

Objective:

  • Try to achieve the highest score possible by keeping the ball bouncing as long as you can.
  • Challenge your reflexes and coordination to master the game!

Enjoy playing the Bounce Ball Game and have fun!
